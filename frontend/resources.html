Quick Start Guide

Get started with Krishisalah in just a few simple steps:

  1. Set up your profile: Enter your farm details, location, and preferences
  2. Choose your crops: Select the crops you want to grow or analyze
  3. Input soil data: Enter your soil test results for accurate recommendations
  4. Monitor weather: Keep track of weather conditions and forecasts
  5. Analyze market: Check market trends and price predictions
  6. Get recommendations: Receive AI-powered crop and market suggestions

Best Practices

Follow these best practices to maximize your farming success:

  1. Regular soil testing: Test your soil every 2-3 years to maintain optimal conditions
  2. Crop rotation: Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup
  3. Water management: Use efficient irrigation methods and monitor water usage
  4. Market timing: Plan your harvest timing based on market price trends
  5. Technology adoption: Use modern farming tools and techniques for better yields
  6. Record keeping: Maintain detailed records of your farming activities and results
